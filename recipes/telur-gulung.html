Telur Gulung

One of the best street food that ever exist

Serving size : probably more than 10pcs
Original source : Liputan6

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Powdered stock
  • Pepper powder
  • 5 tablespoon of water
  • skewers
  • Cooking oil

How to make

  1. Mix water with eggs, add powdered stock and pepper powder
  2. Whisk the eggs until foaming
  3. Turn on the stove and heat up a big amount of cooking oil
  4. Fried the eggs on hot oil with medium fire
  5. Quickly take a skewer and roll it around to stick the egg
  6. Strain it and serve while it's hot
