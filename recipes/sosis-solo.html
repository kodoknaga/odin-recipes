Sosis Solo (Solo Sausage)

Risol-like comfort food

Serving size : around 50pcs
Original source : Mr. Yongki Gunawan's Youtube

Ingredients

  • Chicken filling

    • 100g shallots
    • 60g garlic
    • 40g candlenut
    • 4g coriander
    • 700g chicken breast
    • 1500g water
    • 4 bay leaves
    • 4 lime leaves
    • 40g salt (separate into 2 parts : 30g and 10g)
    • 5g cooking spice
    • 200g coconut milk
    • 15g chicken powder
    • 50gr sugar
    • 5g pepper powder
    • 100g magarin
    • 1 stem of lemongrass

  • Skin wrap

    • 500g tapioka (low-protein) flour
    • 10g chicken powder
    • 50g powdered milk
    • 100g tapioka flour
    • 900g water
    • 5g salt
    • 5g cooking spice
    • 5g egg yolk, 2 egg whites (160g)
    • 100g coconut milk
    • 100g melted margarine

  • Frying

    • 5 whipped eggs
    • Cooking oil

How to make

  • Filling :

    1. Put the chicken breast, bay leaves, lime leaves, cooking spice, and 30gr of salt into a pot and let it boils
    2. While waiting the chicken to boil, blend/pound the shallots, garlic, coriander seeds, candlenut, and a little bit of water until soft
    3. Melt the butter in a pan and add a stem of smashed lemongrass
    4. Add the blended spice into the pan and stir until fragrance. Check on the chicken occasionaly too
    5. After the chicken boils and fully cooked, put it off the pot, then shred the meat with fork
    6. Add the shreded chicken into the pan then add coconut milk, chicken powder, sugar, pepper powder, cooking spice, and a small amount of water from the chicken pot
    7. Stir the pan to mix until the water completely dry

  • Skin :

    1. Put wheat flour, powdered milk, tapioca flour, and chicken powder into a bowl and mix until blended
    2. Add cooking spice, salt, water, egg, and coconut milk. Mix the bowl
    3. Melt the margarine then add it into the bowl and stir it again
    4. Heat up a 15cm pan to use as a mold for the skin wrap
    5. Pour small amount of the mix and spread it around the pan to make it round and thin.
    6. After fully cooked, take it off the pan and let it cool down. Do this for the rest of the batch

  • Wrapping and frying :

    1. Prepare the skin and the filling to shape the sausage
    2. Set 1 sheet of skin on the table and put a small amount of filling on the middle area of the skin wrap
    3. Fold the left and right the skin inside then roll it from top to bottom or vice versa (see video source)
    4. Do this for the rest of the batch
    5. Whisk 5 eggs in a bowl and put the rolled sausage into the eggs to coat it
    6. Heat up the cooking oil and fry the sausage until golden brown
    7. Serve while it's hot
